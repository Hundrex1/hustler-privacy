Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-18

What this app does

Hustler recommends “hustles” based on your profile (skills, location, preferences) and helps you follow a task plan.

Data we collect

  • Account info: if you sign in with Google, we receive your basic account identifier from Google/Supabase Auth.
  • Profile info you enter: skills, country, city, preferences (hours/week, budget, etc.).
  • Usage analytics: app events such as onboarding completed, recommendations refreshed, hustle started, task checked, check-in clicked.

How we use your data

  • To generate and personalize recommendations and tasks.
  • To operate the app and improve features.
  • To understand usage via analytics.

Analytics

We use analytics (e.g., PostHog) to understand how the app is used. Analytics may collect device/app event data.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers that help run the app (authentication, database, analytics).

Data retention

We keep your data as long as you maintain an account or as needed to provide the service.

Your choices

  • You can edit your profile inside the app.
  • You can request deletion by contacting us at: support@yourdomain.com
